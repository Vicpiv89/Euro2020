How does Euro 2020 work?


Qualifying

20 of the teams who participated in this Euro Cup qualified directly from the qualifiers. These were the top 2 teams in each group. The group is played with two matches between each team, and the points are standard:

  • Win - 3 points
  • Draw - 1 point
  • Loss - 0 points
4 more teams qualified via the qualification play-offs.

The teams that have now qualified for the Euro Cup are placed in a draw that determines the group stage. The group stage consists of 6 groups, each with 4 teams. Teams in the group play one game against the others, and the top 2 teams in the group qualify directly for the round of 16. 4 more teams do qualify for the knockout stage. These are the 3rd place teams that gathered the most points in the group stage, leaving 2 eliminated.

From this point, the tournament is a simple one-game elimination bracket. Teams play eachother, and the winners move to the next round. This process repeats until there is 1 team remaining.
